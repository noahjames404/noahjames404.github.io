Kokomeron

Kokomeron is a horror game developed by Maphatar Animation Studio. It's currently available on Android. And we're also aiming for PC & IOS release (sooner or later).

The game starts in the middle of the night in a town surrounded by walls. Martin, who just got into town, was tasked to buy some condiments by his mother, but to get out, he must unlock the four gates on each side of the town in proper order.

It was easier said than done until he found something peculiar far from the reach of his light.

About Us

We're a small group of developers & artists working together, creating fun and thrilling indie games for the Filipino Gaming Community.

Apart from that, we also developed software applications for start-up businesses and corporations.
