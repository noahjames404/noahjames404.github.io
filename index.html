Kokomeron

Kokomeron is a horror game developed by Maphatar Animation Studio. It's currently available on Android. And we're also aiming for PC & IOS release (sooner or later).

The game starts in the middle of the night in a town surrounded by walls. Martin, who just got into town, was tasked to buy some condiments by his mother, but to get out, he must unlock the four gates on each side of the town in proper order.

It was easier said than done until he found something peculiar far from the reach of his light.

FAQS

Does the game support Multiplayer?

  • The game currently does not support multiplayer.

Does the game support IOS and PC versions?

  • The game currently does not support both platforms. But we're planning to do so once we've cleared the issues on the Android version.

Broken Download Links

  • If you can't download any of our links, kindly report them to us - at info@maphatar.com or send us a message on our Facebook Page.

Can't Open APK File

  • From your settings, go to security, and allow Installation of Unknown Apps.

Block by Play Protect

  • Google Playstore is yet to recognize our program. Thus they considered us as "unknown publishers," but fret not, tis us all along. Once they've reviewed our application, Playstore will no longer block our app.

APK not Installed.

  • Check if you have enough space in your mobile phone. If it still wouldn't work, redownload the APK file again from another link.

The Game lags / FPS drop

  • Some players may encounter an FPS drop even with a high-end mobile phone. This issue might have been due to background apps running all at once, causing their CPU to overheat, leading to CPU-Throttling, which results in an FPS drop. Clearing them might improve the performance of the game.
  • Lowering the default Quality Settings in the game will greatly affect the overall performance of the game. (This feature will be available on v0.0.3)

Found a Bug?

  • Send us feedback via survey forms, and we'll fix the issue. Survey forms are commonly shown after your gameplay or below the APK download button.
